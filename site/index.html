About

Rich Source Development is a web solutions studio, based in the lively capital of Bulgaria Sofia. It unites professionals with strong “Can do” attitude backed by abundant practical experience.

We focus on collaboration with digital agencies in need of reliable, domain expert partners. Since our establishment in 2013, we have worked with Bulgarian and foreign agencies in the successful delivery of software solutions for various business scenarios.

Whether you need technical consultation or an expert to join your development team, you can rely on a personal attitude.

Services

We offer software development services specialized in the Microsoft .NET stack and web development. Our primary focus is on back-end architecture and implementation.

Outsorcing

We develop modules and apps for your business or provide software developers who can join your team for a certain project or time period.
We value lasting relationships.

Consulting

We help you grow as we build an architectural plan for a development process or suggest different practices or technologies.
We like learning and sharing.

CMS Solutions

We create solutions powered by modern CMS platforms like Sitecore, Sitefinity, Umbraco and more. We build customized modules and extend your system according to your needs.
We strive for efficiency.
